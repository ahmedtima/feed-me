Feed-Me

1 in 8 people in Nigeria are hungry, And there are people who wish to donate but don't know how to get the food to the poor or don't have the time. Donate Food Now is a means for people who want to help end poverty get access to People who are in need.

Donor means a person or organization that donates food to help others and feed-me is here to help the less priviledged have easier access to the donors.

At Feed-Me, our main goal is to feed 100 people per day thereby reducing the poverty rate in our community. By donating food we're feeding people,supporting undeveloped communities and helping eradicate poverty.
